Who are we?

Women in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin is a non-profit organization dedicated to building a network and community of women. Our mission is to encourage and support women in computing through: outreach, professional development, academic initiatives, and social events.

What is WiCS Hacks?

WiCS Hacks is our annual 2-day hackathon for all women and non-binary students. This year it will be held completely virtually. It's a place for students to learn new skills and come up with creative solutions using technology. To help along the way, we provide mentorship and workshops. Beginners are welcome!
  • Learn new skills and practice problem-solving.
  • Create products and services solving real-world problems.
  • Meet company sponsors and engineers in industry.
